About Ingenious'17
❝

Ingenious'17 is a National level Technical Symposium Conducted by Department of Mechanical Engineering, Rajalakhsmi Engineering College.

❞
Situated in Chennai, Rajalakshmi Engineering College was established in the year 1997 under the aegis of Rajalakshmi Educational Trust whose members have had consummate experience in the fields of education and industry. The Department of Mechanical Engineering has been the flagbearer of brand REC and has consistently proved its mettle to be in the frontiers of academics as well as other activities outside of the routine curriculum. This year, REC is going one notch higher with the Mechanical symposium Ingenious '17.

Come and participate in all the technical and non-technical events to win exciting rewards

Water Rocketry


Water Rocketry
